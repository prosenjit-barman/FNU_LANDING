Why Florida National University

For over 35 years, Florida National University has opened doors for sudents to acquire a hands-on, career-focused education to further their personal and professional goals.

Flexible Class Option

FNU's Semester (16 weeks) is divided into two 8-week terms with students taking two courses each term, andclasses run year round. This allows students to finish their programs (and start their careers) faster than traditional program.

Job Placement Servies

We work with potential employers in the private and public sector. businesses, companies, hospitals and the general community to help our students make the transition from students to professionals in the workforce.

Hands-On Training

For careers that require hands-on learning, our on-campus options provide that critical element to ensure student learning is successful.

Financial Aid and Scholarship Opportunities

Scholarships are availabe. And our staff works with those who need financial aid to ensure allocations are explored for those who qualify.

Active Professors in Their Field

Our Professors are not just instructors, They have been ad continue to be active in their field, such as business, criminal justice, law, nursing, or technology.

Transfer Credits Accepted

Rather than losing credits for classes that you have taken at other higher education insitutions, many can be transferred and count towards your degree. Check with your academic advisor to see how those transferred credit can be applied.

Online Classes

Many students are unable to attend on-campus classes;therefore we offer many degrees completely online. We can provide a combination of on-campus or online courses to accommodate your schedule.
